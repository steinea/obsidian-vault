# Nothing but Thunder

### Michael Ondaatje

[[2015]] / [[commonplace]] / [[In the Skin of a Lion]]

"—They don't want your revolution, Patrick said to Alice. 
—No. They won't be involved. Just you. You're a mongrel, like me. Not like my daughter here. But like me.
—So what do you want?
—Nothing but thunder."
